My Work

Tasteful

JavaScript/JQuery/FireBase

Tasteful is a food search app that provides the user with a personal and custom search for food. Tasteful matches descriptive keywords of the desired food and the user’s personal food preferences creating a refined and precise search experience. Instead of choosing between food suggestions based on the opinions and preferences of others, Tasteful connects the user with options that appeal to their food preferences, desires, and cravings.

Movie Trivia

JavaScript/JQuery

A trivia game that puts your movie knowledge to the test. You're give 30 seconds to correctly guess the answer to the given question. Whether you answer correct or incorrectly, you still recieve a movie clip at the end of each question. But which movie clip you recieve depends on whether you were right or not. At the end of game you're score is tallied and you can either brag to all your friends about how smart you are or just watch the great movies shown to you in the game!

Train Schedule

JavaScript/JQuery/FireBase

Train Schedule is an Application that enables user to input information about a train and view the information on an easy to read table. The table gives you useful information such as the name of the train, the location its headed, when its arriving at the station, and the frequency of said line. Information on the train is data persistant so that even when the Train Scheduler is closed you're easily able to open the app back up and not worry about having to re-input all the train information.
